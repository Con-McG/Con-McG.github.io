Understanding Number Systems

Decimal, Binary, and Hexadecimal

What Are Number Systems?

Number systems represent numbers in different formats. The most common systems are Decimal, Binary, and Hexadecimal.

Binary (Base-2)

The binary system uses only 0 and 1 and is the foundation of computing.

Hexadecimal (Base-16)

The hexadecimal system represents numbers with 0-9 and A-F and is widely used in programming.
